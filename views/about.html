About LYNE

Welcome to Lyne, where passion meets affordability in the world of sportswear. Established in January 2024 by two driven entrepreneurs, Lyne was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to provide quality sporting clothes without breaking the bank.

Our Mission

At Lyne, our mission is clear: to offer top-notch sporting apparel at prices that won't hold you back. We believe that everyone should have access to gear that inspires them to pursue their athletic passions without compromise. Whether you're hitting the trails on your mountain bike, scoring goals on the soccer field, or simply enjoying a leisurely jog through the park, we've got you covered.

Our Journey

Our journey began when two young entrepreneurs, fueled by their love for sports and frustrated by the sky-high prices of athletic wear, decided to take matters into their own hands. With a shared vision of affordability and a commitment to quality, Lyne was born. Since our inception, we've been dedicated to providing a solution to overpriced clothing, ensuring that everyone can gear up for their favorite activities without breaking the bank.

Our Products

At Lyne, we specialize in a range of sporting apparel, including hoodies, tees, and jerseys, designed to cater to athletes of all ages and skill levels. Our products are not only affordable but also crafted with the highest standards of quality in mind. From durable fabrics to stylish designs, every item in our collection reflects our dedication to excellence.
